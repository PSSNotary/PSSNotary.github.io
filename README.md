## About

Pacific Seal Signings provides professional, in-person mobile notary and loan signing services across San Diego County. We travel to you — home, office, hospital, or correctional facility — for general notary work, real estate and loan signings, estate planning documents, and more.

- **Commission No.** 2557176
- **NNA Certified** Loan Signing Agent & Background Screened
- **Bonded & Insured** up to $100,000 (E&O coverage)
- **Service Area:** All of San Diego County, plus San Clemente
- **Availability:** 7 days a week, including evenings and weekends

## Services

- General Notary Work (acknowledgments, jurats, oaths/affirmations)
- Real Estate & Loan Signing Closings (refinances, purchases, HELOCs)
- Estate Planning Documents (Power of Attorney, Healthcare Directives, Trusts)
- Hospital & Care Facility Visits
- Jail & Detention Facility Visits
- Vehicle Title Transfers
- Apostille Preparation

*Note: California-commissioned notaries cannot perform Remote Online Notarization (RON). All signings are conducted in person per California law.*
